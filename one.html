Little Brief
Aditya Institute of Technology and Management is located at K. Kotturu, Tekkali, Srikakulam District, Andhra Pradesh. It is located in an extent of 50 acres of clean, green and serene area. The institute has the beauty of modern day Gurukul. It is situated on the banks of the river Vamsadhara at a short distance and having the sea at an hours journey. The institute is provided with an enjoyable coastal climate. The college is marching ahead under the able guidance of Chairman Rtn (PHF) Dr. K. Someswara Rao who is a Pediatrician by profession and committed to social service with a special reference to technical education with unstinted support from the Secretary Sri. L.L. Naidu, Director Prof. V.V. Nageswara Rao and other members of the Sri Aditya Educational Society. Aditya Institute of Technology and Management currently offers 6 U.G programs in the disciplines of ECE, CSE, IT, EEE, CE and MECH, P.G program in MBA, and 6 disciplines of M. Tech.The total sanctioned intake per year is 840 in B. Tech programmes, 60 in MBA and 156 in M. Tech.
